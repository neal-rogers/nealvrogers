About Me

Some might call me a reserved but charismatic individual with a penchant for sarcasm and dark humor. Others might choose more colorful and unflattering expletives to describe my character. I would like to think that the real me is somewhere in the middle.

For a time (about a decade, in fact) I worked in the information technology field, both in the public and private sectors.It’s with a renewed sense of purpose that I hope the City That Never Sleeps will continue to inspire me, to urge me to create, and guilt me into pursuing what I truly love...
