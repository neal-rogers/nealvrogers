About Me

For a time (about a decade, in fact) I worked in IT, both in the public and private sectors. These days I'm relying on the City That Never Sleeps to inspire me, to urge me to create.

Some might call me a reserved but charismatic individual with a penchant for sarcasm and dark humor. Others might choose more colorful, unflattering expletives to describe my character. I would like to think that the real me is somewhere in the middle.
