Daka Production Co.

Daka Production Co. is an independent music studio based in Bangladesh. As a relatively new and youthful company, the client expressed a need to reflect a sense of maturity within their brand. Influences included street art as well as traditional Islamic color themes and sensibilities.

Redline Fitness

Redlines Fitness specializes in focused, instructor-led fitness routines guided by sound principles meant to empower individuals as athletes. As a program with a diverse following across age and gender, Redline needed a logo that matched their popular, high intensity weightlifting and gymnastics conditioning regimen. Straying from the tropes of the typical distressed or rugged aesthetic, simple, precise forms were reflective of the of Redline Fitness philosophy toward health and wellness.

Fastrax Productions

Fastrax Productions is a small record label and production studio out of the Midwest. With their headquarters in St. Louis, Missouri and a satellite studio in Atlanta, Georgia, Fastrax Productions is renowned for their distinctive, St. Louis hip-hop vibe. The client took pride in their recruitment and promotion of up-and-coming artists through meticulous and precisely curated style conveyed through their visual identity.
