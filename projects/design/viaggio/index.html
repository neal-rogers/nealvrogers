User Research

Some key insights from user interviews reflected the desire for

  • “Being able to make my own way, to explore, is crucial.”

  • “I expect a smooth, premium experience when I travel.”

  • “Entering personal info or payment details can be tedious.”

Additional user insights...

  • Lack of confidence managing travel documents with a device.

  • Social media “check-in” is often incentivized.

  • Communicating across multiple media/channels is common.

  • Changes to arrangements typically require contact with a representative.
